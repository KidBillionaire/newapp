How Bluff Royale Works

Bluff Royale brings a unique twist to online poker by combining FaceTime video interaction with real-money sweepstakes. Here’s how it works:

Step 1: Sign Up

Create your account quickly and easily. All you need is an email address and a password to get started.

Step 2: Join a Table

Once signed in, browse through available poker tables. You can join a table that fits your style and start playing with other real players via live video.

Step 3: FaceTime Poker

Engage with other players in real-time using FaceTime-style video feeds. Bluff, read your opponents, and enjoy the thrill of a real poker experience from anywhere.

Step 4: Win Big

Participate in real-money sweepstakes as you play. Every game is an opportunity to win cash prizes and take your poker skills to the next level!
